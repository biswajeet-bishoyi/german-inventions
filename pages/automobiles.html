The Birth of Modern Automobiles

The automobile, as we know it today, was revolutionized by Karl Benz in 1885. His invention of the first gasoline-powered car was a monumental step in the history of transportation. The Benz Patent-Motorwagen is considered the first true automobile, setting the foundation for the modern automotive industry.
